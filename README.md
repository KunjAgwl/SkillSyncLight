**JobFinder AI** is your retro-futuristic job-hunting terminal. Drop in your PDF character sheet (resume) and our embedded `TF-IDF` semantic algorithm cross-references your skills against the JSON job listing database. No bloated servers. No databases to install. Just pure local NLP computing power.
